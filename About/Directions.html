Directions

From I-40 West

  1. Follow I-40 West and exit off ramp 273A for NC 54 W toward Chapel Hill.
  2. Continue on 54 W for 2.9 miles.
  3. Continue onto Raleigh Road for 0.8 miles.
  4. Turn right onto Country Club Road for 0.3 miles.
  5. Continue onto E Cameron Avenue for 0.4 miles.
  6. Chapman Hall will be on your left (South) behind Phillips Hall.

From I-40 East

  1. Follow I-40 East and exit off ramp 266 for NC 86 S toward Chapel Hill.
  2. Continue on 86 S/Martin Luther King Jr Blvd for 3.8 miles.
  3. Slight left onto N Columbia Street for 0.3 miles.
  4. Turn left onto E Cameron Avenue for 500 feet.
  5. Chapman Hall will be on your right (South) behind Phillips Hall.
